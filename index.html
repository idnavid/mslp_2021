About the MLSP Workshop

The 31st MLSP workshop in the series of workshops organized by the IEEE Signal Processing Society MLSP Technical Committee will take place at the University of Melboune, Australia, in a fully virtual program due to the pandemic, and present the most recent and exciting advances in machine learning for signal processing through keynote talks, tutorials, special and regular single-track sessions as well as matchmaking events. The presented papers will be published in and indexed by IEEE Xplore.

Access the online platform: https://mlsp-virtual.org/

Registered participants can log in using their credentials that are sent out over email on the first morning of the conference. The conference is live streamed over Zoom. The online platform also hosts the accepted papers, presentation videos, and lets the participants post questions to paper authors. If you experience any problems with logging into the online platform, contact Conference Catalysts.
